About Me

Hello! I'm Ömer, a Software Engineer at Dronetools in Sevilla, Spain. I specialize in engineering high-performance flight control and autonomous navigation systems for drones using C++, while developing data processing pipelines and user-facing applications with Python.

I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering at Duzce University (expected May 2025), with a study abroad experience at the University of Sevilla. My professional journey includes IT internship experience at Goodyear and significant contributions to the RoboTaxi autonomous vehicle competition, where our team achieved top rankings for consecutive years.

My recent projects include an Online PDF Editor developed with Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS as my university graduation project, and continued work on autonomous systems development.
